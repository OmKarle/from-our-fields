Willowbrook Farm

4.9 (218) 3.2 mi · Purcellville, VA

The Harrelsons have been farming the same 40 acres outside Purcellville since 1968. Third-generation farmer Rachel Harrelson runs the operation today, growing heirloom tomatoes, peppers, and leafy greens on no-till beds fed only with compost from their own livestock.

Farming practices

🌱
No synthetic pesticidesPest management via companion planting and beneficial insects.
🪱
No-till soil managementBuilds organic matter and protects soil microbiology season after season.
💧
Drip irrigation from well waterReduces water use by ~60% compared to overhead systems.
🐝
On-site pollinator habitat2 acres dedicated to native wildflowers and native bee hives.
